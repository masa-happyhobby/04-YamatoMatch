How it works

  • Play: Match the left “Word” with the right “Meaning”.
  • Review: Tap a cleared word to open details (nuance + example sentences).
  • Listen: Use the pronunciation button (ja-JP text-to-speech).
  • Practice: Open the Pronunciation list and tap the speaker on the right for quick drills.
